FAQ

We understand that businesses often have specific concerns when it comes to IT services and software solutions. Here are some of the most common questions we receive

What types of businesses do you work with?

We primarily support small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and startups, helping them integrate software, optimize IT infrastructure, and strengthen cybersecurity. Our services are tailored to businesses in various industries, including retail, healthcare, technology, and professional services.

Do you offer software recommendations, or only resell licenses?

We do both. Our IT consultants work with businesses to identify the best software solutions based on operational needs and goals. We then provide licensed software with seamless integration and expert support.

How do you ensure data security when integrating software?

Security is a top priority. Our API development and automation solutions follow industry best practices to ensure data protection. Additionally, our cybersecurity services help businesses safeguard sensitive information against potential threats.

What level of IT support do you provide after implementation?

We offer ongoing IT support, including system monitoring, troubleshooting, updates, and optimization. Our goal is to ensure that your IT infrastructure and software remain efficient and secure in the long run.

How do I know if my business needs IT consulting?

If you're struggling with outdated software, inefficient workflows, or security concerns, IT consulting can help. Our team assesses your IT landscape, identifies areas for improvement, and recommends custom solutions that align with your business objectives.
